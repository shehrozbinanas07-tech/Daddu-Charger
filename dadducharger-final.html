About Daddu Charger

Daddu Charger Gaming Store is Pakistan's premier destination for custom gaming PCs and peripherals, based at Royal Plaza, 6th Road, Satellite Town, Rawalpindi.

Founded in 2019, we grew from a small tech accessories shop into a full-scale gaming store with 340,000+ social media followers and thousands of satisfied customers nationwide.

We specialise in custom gaming PC builds — from budget 245K setups to 1.4M+ flagship workstations. Every build is assembled in-house and rigorously tested before dispatch.
